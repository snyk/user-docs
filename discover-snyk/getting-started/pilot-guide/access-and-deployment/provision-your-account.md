# Provision your account

{% include "../../../.gitbook/includes/pilot-guide-navigation.md" %}

Provide your Snyk account team with the email of the desired Snyk tenant admin.

Your account team provisions your Snyk tenant, which you can access after you receive a welcome email from Snyk. For more details on this process, see the [Auto-provisioning guide](../../../implementation-and-setup/enterprise-setup/auto-provisioning-guide.md).
