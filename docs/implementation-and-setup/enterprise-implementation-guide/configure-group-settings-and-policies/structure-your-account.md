# Structure your account

## Plan your Group structure

{% hint style="success" %}
**Key decision:** Determine how many top-level accounts your company requires. Most organizations need only one.
{% endhint %}

The Snyk Group is the highest administrative level you manage.

* **Default:** Most customers use one Snyk Group representing their company name.
* **Large enterprises:** Use multiple Groups only if you must keep business units or subsidiaries completely isolated.
