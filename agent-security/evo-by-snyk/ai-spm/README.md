# AI-SPM

AI Security Posture Management (AI-SPM) gives you visibility, governance, and security oversight of the AI assets in your code. It maps your assets, scores their risk, and helps you enforce governance, so you can maintain compliance and make informed decisions about your AI infrastructure.

{% hint style="info" %}
AI-SPM assets appear under **Inventory** > **Code**.
{% endhint %}

### How AI-SPM works

AI-SPM runs as three agents:

* Map assets: the **Discovery agent** maps the AI assets across your repositories and applications. To learn more, visit [AI asset visibility](ai-asset-visibility.md).
* Assess risk: the **Risk intelligence agent** scores your assets and evaluates your policies to surface violations and risky usage. To learn more, visit [Risk intelligence](risk-intelligence.md).
* Enforce governance: the **Policy agent** governs your assets through policies and issues. To learn more, visit [Policies and issues](../platform-surfaces/policies-and-issues.md).

### Set up AI-SPM

Enable Evo, then import the repositories you want AI-SPM to scan.

To enable Evo, visit [Access and authentication](../access-and-authentication.md). To import repositories, visit [AI asset visibility](ai-asset-visibility.md).
