# Platform surfaces

Evo organizes the product into four main surfaces:

* [Inventory](https://app.gitbook.com/s/N5N885PkllOWeBmgm3Bp/evo-by-snyk/platform-surfaces/inventory):  The AI assets discovered across your environment, with their assessed risk.
* [Policies & issues](https://app.gitbook.com/s/N5N885PkllOWeBmgm3Bp/evo-by-snyk/platform-surfaces/policies-and-issues): The rules that govern AI usage and the issues raised when assets violate them.
* [Reports](https://app.gitbook.com/s/N5N885PkllOWeBmgm3Bp/evo-by-snyk/platform-surfaces/reports): How your AI security posture changes over time.
* [Evo chat](https://app.gitbook.com/s/N5N885PkllOWeBmgm3Bp/evo-by-snyk/platform-surfaces/evo-chat): A conversational way to explore your data and take action.
